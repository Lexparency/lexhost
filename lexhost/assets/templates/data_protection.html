I. Erhebung und Speicherung personenbezogener Daten

Bei dem Aufruf dieser Website werden durch den Browser der auf Ihrem Endgerät (z.B. Computer, Tablet, Smartphone etc.) verwendet wird, automatisch Informationen an den Server gesendet auf dem unsere Webanwendung gehostet ist. Art und Zweck der Verwendung personenbezogener Daten

Diese Informationen werden technisch ohne Ihr Zutun erfasst und bis zur automatisierten Löschung gespeichert. II. Cookies

Diese Webanwendung verwendet Cookies zur anonymisierten Reichweitenmessung, die entweder von unserem Server oder dem Server Dritter an den Browser des Nutzers übertragen werden. Bei Cookies handelt es sich um kleine Dateien, welche auf Ihrem Endgerät gespeichert werden. Ihr Browser greift auf diese Dateien zu. Durch den Einsatz von Cookies erhöht sich die Benutzerfreundlichkeit und Sicherheit dieser Website. Durch das Setzen eines Cookies erhalten wir keine unmittelbare Kenntnis von Ihrer Identität.

Gängige Browser bieten die Einstellungsoption, Cookies nicht zuzulassen. Hinweis: Es ist nicht gewährleistet, dass Sie auf alle Funktionen dieser Website ohne Einschränkungen zugreifen können, wenn Sie entsprechende Einstellungen vornehmen.

Data protection

With this privacy policy, we want to give you (i.e., as a user, prospect, and / or visitor) information on the nature, extent, and purpose of processing personal information through our online presence.

I. Collection / storage of personal data / nature and purpose of their use

When this website is accessed, the browser used on your device (for example, computer, tablet, smartphone, etc.) automatically sends information to the server on which our website is stored. This information is temporarily stored in a so-called logfile (log file).

These are the following information:

  • IP address of the requesting computer (pseudonymised)
  • Date and time of access,
  • Website from which access is made (referrer URL),
  • Name and URL of the retrieved file,
  • used browser and possibly the operating system of your computer.

This information is collected technically without your intervention and stored until automated deletion. We process this data to ensure a smooth connection and convenient use of our website, to evaluate system security and stability, and for administrative purposes.

Data processing is therefore based on a legitimate interest on our part (legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR), which follows the aforementioned data collection purposes. We do not use the data collected for the purpose of obtaining inferences about your person.

II. Cookies

Cookies are used on this website. Cookies are small files that your browser or Internet access program automatically creates and that are stored on your device (laptop, tablet, smartphone, etc.) when you visit our site. Cookies do not contain viruses, Trojans or malicious software. A cookie is used to store information that relates to the specific device you are using. By setting a cookie, we do not receive any direct knowledge of your identity.

The use of cookies on this website serves on the one hand to make the use of it more pleasant for you. So-called session cookies can be used so far to recognize that you have already visited individual pages of our website. These are automatically deleted after leaving our page. On the other hand, we use temporary cookies to optimize usability. These cookies are stored on your device for a certain period of time. If you visit our site again to take advantage of the services offered, you will automatically be aware that you have already been with us and about what inputs and settings you have made in order not to have to re-enter them.

The data processed by cookies are required for the purposes mentioned to safeguard our legitimate interests as well as those of third parties (legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR).

Prevent Cookie Storage: Most browsers accept cookies automatically. However, you can configure your browser so that no cookies are stored on your computer, or always a hint appears before a new cookie is created. However, disabling cookies completely may mean that you can not use all features of our website. You can find information on the corresponding settings in the documentation for your Internet access software (browser).
